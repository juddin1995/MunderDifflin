Our Story

We are the best paper company in the world. We are Munder Difflin. Paper made by people. The Munder Difflin guarantee.

Munder Difflin first opened its doors in Scranton, Pennsylvania in 1969. Since then we have expanded internationally. Our paper products are enjoyed worldwide.
